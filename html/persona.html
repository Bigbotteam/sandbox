Would you like to change your MoneyStyle?

There are no good or bad MoneyStyles. We are what we are and our bias when it comes to love, money, career, and other life choices are shaped by some combination of nature and nurture. Similarly, as with most things in life, some balance is healthy. Wherever you find yourself in the MoneyStyle chart, you can pick up and learn behaviours from other MoneyStyles to complement your natural tendencies. For example, if you are an extreme Scrooge, getting yourself to live (and spend) a little, might be a good idea.

However, you should bear in mind that the most diametrically opposed styles are on opposite diagonals of the MoneyStyle chart. These styles are the hardest to transition between. For example, it is more difficult to transition from being an Avoider to being an Amasser, than it is to transition from being an Avoider to a Spender. This is because, for these transformations to occur, significant change is required on both dimensions (emotional and time orientation).
