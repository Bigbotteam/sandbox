What is the advice for each money style as individuals?

Individual Money Style: There are no good or bad money styles. We are what we are and our bias when it comes to love, money, career, and other life choices are shaped by some combination of nature and nurture. Similarly, as with most things in life, some balance is healthy. Wherever you find yourself in the chart, you can pick up and learn behaviours from other Money Styles to complement your natural tendencies. For example, if you are an extreme Scrooge, getting yourself to live (and spend) a little, might be a good idea However, you should bear in mind that the most diametrically opposed styles are on opposite diagonals of the Money Style Chart. These styles are the hardest to transition between. For example, it is more difficult to transition from being a Dodger to being a Collector, than it is to transition from being a Dodger to a Splurger. This is because, for these transformations to occur, significant change is required on both dimensions (emotional and time orientation)
